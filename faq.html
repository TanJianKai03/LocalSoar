Faq

Common questions about traveling in Malaysia

What is the best time to visit Malaysia?
Malaysia is a year-round destination, but the best time is generally between March and early October to avoid the heaviest monsoon rains on the east coast.
Do I need a visa to enter?
Many nationalities receive a visa-free entry for 30 to 90 days. However, all travelers must complete the Malaysia Digital Arrival Card (MDAC) within three days before arrival.
Is it safe to travel solo in Malaysia?
Yes, Malaysia is considered one of the safest countries in Southeast Asia for solo travelers. English is widely spoken, making navigation very easy.
What should I pack?
Light, breathable clothing is best for the humid heat. However, bring a light jacket for shopping malls or buses where the AC can be quite cold!
