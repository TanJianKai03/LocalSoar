Guide

Kuala Lumpur
Kuala Lumpur is the capital city of Malaysia. It is known for its modern skyline and cultural diversity.
Kelantan
Kelantan is located in the northeast of Peninsular Malaysia. It is famous for its traditional Malay culture and Islamic values.
Penang
Penang is a popular island state on the west coast of Malaysia. It is famous for its street food and cultural heritage.
Malacca
Melaka is a historic state with a long colonial past. It is well known for its heritage sites and multicultural history
Sabah
A paradise for nature and adventure. Sabah features Mount Kinabalu, crystal-clear islands, rich wildlife, and unforgettable sunrise views.
Sarawak
Sarawak is the largest state in Malaysia by land area. It is known for its rainforests, rivers, and diverse ethnic groups.
