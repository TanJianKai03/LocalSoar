Pantai Cahaya Bulan

Pantai Cahaya Bulan, often called PCB, is one of Kelantan’s most well-known beaches and a favourite spot among locals and visitors seeking a peaceful escape. Stretching along the northeastern coast, this beach is known for its wide sandy shoreline, gentle sea breeze, and calm waves, making it an ideal place for relaxing walks, picnics, and enjoying beautiful sunsets. The atmosphere is laid-back and refreshing, offering a perfect break from the busy life of Kota Bharu, which is only a short drive away.

The beach area is lined with local food stalls and small cafés where you can try Kelantanese snacks such as keropok lekor, coconut drinks, and fresh seafood grilled by local vendors. During weekends and holidays, families often gather here for picnics, kite flying, and casual beach games. Although the waves are usually strong and not suitable for swimming, the beach remains popular for its scenic views and cooling sea wind.

Nearby, visitors can explore local craft shops, batik outlets, and small markets that sell handmade souvenirs unique to Kelantan. The combination of natural beauty, cultural charm, and easy accessibility makes Pantai Cahaya Bulan a must-visit destination for anyone travelling to Kelantan. Whether you’re here to unwind, enjoy the coastal scenery, or capture stunning photos, PCB offers a simple yet memorable seaside experience.

Location:

Pantai Cahaya Bulan, 15350 Kota Bharu, Kelantan, Malaysia
